Motivation

In this assignment, I looked at the traits of three different penguin species (Adelie, Chinstrap, and Gentoo). Studying the physical characteristics of penguins and the differences across species is very fascinating, and learning more about the natural world around is very important.

Data

I selected this dataset because I am a large fan of the NFL, and I enjoy looking at the statistics of the games, as well as the monetary side of signing players. This dataset comes from https://data.world/dataremixed/nfl-players-stats, which is a data collection website. It is a collection of the top paid NFL players in 2017, as well as their positional data. With this data, I wanted to see if there was any correlation between the positions that players play and the amount of money, or "cap hit" they take up. I know that quarterbacks, or QBs, likely make the most money because their position is the most important, but I wanted to see if there was any correlation between the other positions and the amount of money they make. To clean the data, I made the rank of players with null values (meaning they are tied in rank) as the same rank as the player above them. In conclusion, I chose this dataset because I am interested in the NFL and I wanted to see if there was any correlation between the positions of players and the amount of money they make.

Data Visualization(s)

NFL Player Cap Hit vs Position and Team

For this visualization, I chose to create a heatmap in Altair. The purpose of this visualization is to explore the relationship between the position of a player on a team and their average cap hit. This visualization uses the marks of areas, and the channels of position and color saturation. To encode this information, I used the x encoding to show the team names along the horizontal axis and the y encoding to show the position names along the vertical axis. The color encoding is used to show the average cap hit for each combination of team and position, with a color scale that ranges from light to dark red.I chose the red color scheme because it is associated with passion, excitement, and energy, which are all attributes that are commonly associated with sports. I also chose to use a diverging color scale to highlight the differences in cap hit values between the different teams and positions. Overall, I believe that this visualization effectively communicates the relationship between a player's position on a team and their average cap hit. By using color to highlight the differences between different team and position combinations, this visualization makes it easy for the viewer to quickly identify which teams and positions have the highest and lowest average cap hits. Inspiration is from https://altair-viz.github.io/gallery/simple_heatmap.html.

NFL Player Cap Hit vs Ranking

In this visualization, I created a scatterplot that plots the Cap Hit of each player against their ranking. I chose to use a scatterplot because it allows me to visualize the relationship between the Cap Hit and the ranking of each player. This visualization uses the marks of points and the channels of position and color hue. The different color hues allow the user to see how much a position is being paid when not filtering by position. I made the scatterplot interactive by including a dropdown menu that allows the user to filter the data by position. This allows the user to explore the data more deeply and see specific values for each data point. I also added the interactibility of scrolling throughout the plot and zooming in, allowing users a clearer view of points that may be overlapping. Each point also has an interactive tooltip to allow the user to understand the value of each point. In conclusion, I believe that this visualization effectively communicates the relationship between the Cap Hit and the ranking of each player. Inspiration is from https://altair-viz.github.io/gallery/scatter_tooltips.html.
